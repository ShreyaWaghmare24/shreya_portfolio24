About Me

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Science with Honors in Cybersecurity.

My focus lies in digital forensics.I’m drawn to problems that don’t have immediate answers and systems that don’t want to be understood.

Outside the cyberworld, I’m someone who sketches when thoughts feel too loud, reads when I want to meet minds unlike mine, and often loses track of time exploring human behavior through the lens of social psychology. I believe the best investigators are curious about both people and machines — and I try to be both.
